Frequently Asked Questions.

Why wouldn 't I just hire a full-time designer, freelancer, or agency?

Good question. For starters, the annual cost of a full-time senior-level designer now exceeds $100,000, plus benefits (and good luck finding one available). Freelancers can also be a mixed bag – price and quality varies. Agencies do solid work, but often times at a premium to both your time and actually working budgets. Our flat-fee subscription plan isn’t just predictable, it’s incredibly practical. That means you get exactly what you need, when you need it most.

Is there a limit to how many requests I can have?

Once you’ve subscribed, you 're able to add as many design requests to your queue as you 'd like, and they will be delivered one request at a time..

What programs do you design in?

Although most of our design requests are done in Figma, we do utilize the Adobe Creative Suite for delivering print-ready mechanical files. For presentation design work, we work in Keynote.

How do I request designs?

Once subscribed, you will be given access to a shared Trello board for submitting any and all design requests. How you choose to brief us from there is entirely up to you – you can link to a Google doc with a written brief, provide wireframes, or even record a fast Loom video (for those who prefer not to write their briefs out). Simply put, if it can be linked to or shared in Trello, it 's fair game.

How fast will I receive my design work?

Once work begins, on average, most requests are completed in just two days or less. However, more complex requests can take longer.

What if I only have a single request?

That 's fine. You can pause your subscription when finished. We operate on a month-to-month basis. No long-term contracts are required.

Are there any refunds if I don 't like the service?

Unfortunately due to the high quality nature of our work, no refunds will be issued.
